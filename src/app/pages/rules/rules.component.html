General Rules

- Only Pokémon with 7-letter names appear in all Wordle modes.

- All Wordle rounds allow a maximum of 6 guesses.

- Special characters and numbers are not allowed, so Pokémon such as Farfetch'd, Mr. Mime or Jangmo-o are excluded.

- Mega Evolutions, Primal Forms, Gigantamax, Regional Variants, Paradox Pokémon, and special forms (e.g., Cosplay Pikachu) are not included.

- There is no time limit in any region. Time constraints only apply during the Masters Seven Tournament.

Quick

- Play as many times as you want, with all regions currently available.

Levels

- Progress through the regions in order, from the first to the last, to qualify for the Masters Seven Tournament

- Each region is unlocked after winning 8 Wordle rounds, representing the 8 Pokémon Gyms.

- There is no traditional Pokémon League here. Instead, after defeating Paldea, you gain access to the — Masters Seven Tournament — a special challenge where you'll face Wordles inspired by the most iconic Pokémon trainers. In this mode, hints are disabled, and you’ll have 1 minute per champion to guess one of their Pokémon, selected from any Pokémon used by them or by the Elite Four of their respective region in the games or anime — including the pre-evolutions of those Pokémon.

- The Orange League is not included.

- Each region will exclusively include Pokémon native to that region. For example, Pikachu will only appear in the Kanto region, and Weavile will only appear in Sinnoh.

- Each gym victory (Wordle round) grants up to 100 points, decreasing by 10 points per failed guess and 30 points if a hint is used.

- All regions are replayable, allowing you to farm points and climb the Global Pokémon Trainer Leaderboard.
